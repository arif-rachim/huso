## 🎯 Assessment Strategy

### Daily Quizzes (5 questions each):
- **Format**: Multiple choice (A, B, C, D, E)
- **Scoring**: +1 correct, -0.25 incorrect, 0 blank
- **Time**: 10 minutes maximum
- **Immediate feedback** with explanations

### Weekly Tests (25 questions):
- **Mixed subjects** from week's topics
- **45-minute** time limit
- **HUSO format** simulation
- **Performance analytics** & weak area identification

### Mock Examinations (Full 3-hour format):
- **All 5 subjects** (Biology, Physics, Chemistry, Earth Science, Math)
- **150 total questions** (30 per subject)
- **Scaled scoring** system (drop lowest subject)
- **Complete answer explanations**
